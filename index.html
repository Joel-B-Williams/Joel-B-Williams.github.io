Alas, you were promised muffins

Blueberry
Dry Ingredients
Flour, sifted 1 1/2 cup
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Wet Ingredients
Oil 1/3 cup
Milk 1/3 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Egg 1 each
Final Touches
Blueberries 1 cup

- Combine dry ingredients in a bowl

- Combine wet ingredients in a bowl - pour over dry

- Mix gently with spatula until 90% combined

- Add blueberries

- Mix gently & let rest for 10 minutes

- Bake 350 for 20-25 minutes

- Devour with authority
