Engaging zombies

Combat with zombies is something that, while to be generally avoided, is likely inevitable. Remember that in all cases destruction of the brain is essential.

One-on-one combat

In scenarios where you are facing an individual, or even a small group of zombies, the odds are in your favor. Zombies are slow and clumbsy, and rely on overwhelming numbers to overcome their target.

  • At all times, stay calm
  • Evaulate the situation
  • Eliminate your targets in order of priority

Remember that it is not always necessary to engage - if you can safely bypass without direct confrontation, it is usually safest to do so.

Fighting a horde

In scenarios where you are overwhelmed, it is important to stay calm and evaluate the situation. Identify the nearest point of egress and make your way there immediately

  • Identify the nearest safe area
  • Find the path of least resistance
  • Reevaluate action

Remember - fighting a horde is about escaping the horde. Actually destroying zombies in this number requires mass arms and ammunitions supply that most people simply will not have access to.
