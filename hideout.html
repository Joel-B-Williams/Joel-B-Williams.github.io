The keys to a safe and secure home territory

There are two main things to consider when looking for a functional safe haven during the zombie apocalypse: Security, and Supplies.

Security

Securing a zone from zombies is a unique lesson in safety. Zombies are slow, shambling creatures, but en masse can break through many types of barricades. Use their natural weaknesses against them.

  • Locate above ground level
  • Use ladders/ropes/etc to climb, not ramps or stairs
  • Close off all ground-level entrances

Supplies

A well-stocked home base is essential to survival. Remember the basics - food, water, and warmth.

  • A supply of non-perishibles is essential
  • A clean water supply is even more important
